TockyVN Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their TockyVN account and associated data by contacting us at:

hotro@tocky.vn

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your account, please send an email to the address above and include the email address used for your TockyVN account.

Data that will be deleted

After verification, we will delete your account and associated personal data, including:

  • Profile information
  • Family tree data
  • Member data
  • Photos and media files
  • App activity data

Data retention

Some information may be retained if required for legal, security, or fraud prevention reasons.
